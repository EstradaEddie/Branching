Strawberry Dump Cake

by Grace Tenny

Ingredients

  • 1 box of Yellow Cake mix
  • 2 cans of Strawberry pie mix
  • 1 stick of butter

Directions

Step 1: Heat oven to 350 degrees and grease a 11x13 in glass pan.

Step 2: Pour cans of pie mix into the pan.

Step 3: Pour the bag of cake mix on top of the pie mix. Use a spoon to pat down the cake mix until firm and evenly spread over pie mix.

Step 4: Slice the stick of butter into small slices and place on top of the cake mix. Cover the cake mix with slices of butter.

Step 5: Bake in the oven for approximately 30 - 60 minutes until the cake mix is golden brown.

Step 6: Enjoy while warm, best with some vanilla ice cream!
