Pancakes

by Reginald F

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter or vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)

Directions

  • In a mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  • In another bowl, whisk the egg, and then add the milk, melted butter (or oil), and vanilla extract (if using). Mix well.
  • Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Be careful not to overmix; a few lumps in the batter are okay.
  • Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium-high heat and lightly grease it with oil or butter.
  • Pour 1/4 cup portions of the pancake batter onto the hot skillet for each pancake. You can make them larger or smaller depending on your preference.
  • Cook until bubbles form on the surface of the pancake, then flip it with a spatula and cook the other side until it's golden brown.
  • Transfer the cooked pancakes to a plate and keep them warm.
  • Repeat the process with the remaining batter.
